Promotion Rules

How deals work on PromoFinder

These rules explain how promotions listed on PromoFinder should be used and shared.

  1. Third-party offers only. PromoFinder does not sell products. Every promotion belongs to the original store or brand.
  2. No guarantee of validity. We try to show fresh, working deals, but codes can expire or change at any time.
  3. Check details before checkout. Always confirm price, shipping, and conditions on the store's checkout page.
  4. Honest submissions only. When you share a promotion, you confirm it is real, safe to click, and not misleading.
  5. Affiliate links. Some links may be affiliate links. If you buy, PromoFinder may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
Spot an expired or fake promo? Report it directly on the deal page so we can keep the community clean.

Rules of Website

How to use PromoFinder

Basic rules that keep PromoFinder helpful and respectful for everyone.

  • Respectful behaviour. No hate speech, harassment, spam, or misleading content is allowed anywhere on the site.
  • Account safety. Keep your login details private. You're responsible for activity done through your account.
  • No automated scraping. Bots or unauthorized tools that copy our data or overload our servers are not allowed.
  • Respect intellectual property. Do not upload content you don't have rights to, and don't copy PromoFinder's branding without permission.
Serious or repeated violations may lead to suspension or permanent removal of your PromoFinder account.
